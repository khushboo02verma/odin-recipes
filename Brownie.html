Choco-Brownie


RECIPE

    Ingredients:

    1. 1 (15.25 ounce) cake mix
    2. 1 (3.9 ounce) package instant chocolate pudding mix
    3. 4 large eggs
    4. 1 cup cream
    5. ½ cup vegetable oil
    6. ½ cup water
    7. 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

    Preparation:

    1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
    2. Grease and flour a 10-inch Bundt pan. Have all ingredients at room temperature.
    3. Stir cake mix and pudding mix together in a large bowl.
    4. Make a well in the center and pour in eggs,cream, oil and water.
    5. Beat on low speed until blended.
    6. Scrape bowl, and beat 4 minutes on medium speed.
    7. Stir in chocolate chips. Pour batter into the prepared pan.
    8. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean, about 50 to 60 minutes.
    9. enjoy the brownie!
